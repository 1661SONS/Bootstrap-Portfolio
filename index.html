About Me

I'm Julia - a 28 year-old User Experience Designer from Atlanta. As an avid reader and writer I'm constantly learning new things. When I don't have my nose in a book, you can catch me booking a flight someplace new or resisting the urge to buy new sneakers.

I'm a proud alumna of Georgia Southern University, class of 2013, General Assembly, class of 2014 and 2017, and soon to be an alumna of Georgia Tech, class of 2018 (however, #HailSouthern).

My biggest accomplishment thusfar is winning an EPA grant for a biodiesel engine design which challenged the stigma of 'dirty diesel' while studying mechanical engineering at Georgia Southern. My biggest failure is having a Jeep O|||||||O for 1.5 years and never taking it offroad.
